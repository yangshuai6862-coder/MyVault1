### 8. **Commission for you**

The **Commission** you receive: 20% of the profit from customer purchases.

**Commission Calculation Formula**:

Commission = User's payment * (1 - Apple's commission) * Commission Rate

= $6.0 * (1 - 0.22) * 0.2

= $6.0 * 0.78 * 0.2 = $0.936

Where:

User's payment = Original price * 0.5 Discount = $12 * 0.5 = $6.0

Apple's commission: 0.22 (average in all countries is 22%)

Commission Rate: 0.2 (20%)

So, for each purchase, you will receive $0.936, and refunded purchases will not be counted as valid purchases.
